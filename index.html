What is SmartDialysis?

SmartDialysis is a startup company based in Rochester, New York, USA, with members whose backgrounds vary from biomedical engineering, nephrology, mechanical engineering, business management, and computer science. Our team has been working closely with our partners in the hemodialysis industry.


Our mission is to improve the quality of life and length of End Stage Renal Disease (ESRD) patients with several generations of portable hemodialysis products. Our ultimate goal is to create a portable, iPhone-size hemodialysis device so that ESRD patients will be able to make fewer trips to a dialysis center.


Our product will allow patients to perform hemodialysis at their own convinience with fewer medical complications and an overall better outcome. We are enhancing the healthcare system to improve the overall public health with a limited budget.
